# Part A – Case Study: UPI / Online Payment Fraud

UPI fraud is when someone tricks you into sending money through apps like Google Pay, PhonePe or Paytm. This kind of thing is happening often because lots of people are using online payments every day.
It usually starts with a phone call or a message. The person on the end might say they are from your bank or customer support. They try to scare you by saying there is a problem with your account or they try to make you happy by saying you won a prize.
After they gain your trust they ask for things like your OTP, UPI PIN or bank information. Sometimes they send you a payment request that looks real or a link that seems okay. If you say yes or give them the details your money is gone away.
Students are the ones who get targeted a lot because they use these apps all the time and might do things quickly without checking.
This can cause you to lose money and feel really stressed.. It is not easy to report what happened or get your money back.
