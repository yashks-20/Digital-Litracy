Part B – Stay Safe Online Checklist for College Students in India

* Do not give your UPI PIN, OTP or bank details to anyone

* Make sure you know who is calling or messaging you

* Only use apps that you get from stores

* Do not click on links that you do not know

* Turn on extra security, like two-factor authentication

* Check what is happening with your money all the time

* Check everything before you make a payment

* Keep your phone working properly and updated
